Q1. How to book a cheap flight in Nigeria?

Start by searching for flights online and comparing prices from different airlines. You can use travel websites like Wakanow to compare prices and find the best deals. Next, once you’ve found the cheapest flight, book it online. Make sure to double-check all of the information before confirming your booking. Pay for the flight using a credit card or other secure payment method. As soon as your payment clears, you’ll get a confirmation email containing your flight details. Make sure to keep this email in a safe place. Next, print out your ticket or boarding pass and bring it with you to the airport.
